1. User Responsibilities

1.1 Administrators

  • Maintain system security and integrity
  • Manage employee accounts and permissions
  • Approve/Reject reservation requests
  • Monitor compliance with hotel policies

1.2 Employees

  • Respond to service requests within 24 hours
  • Update request statuses promptly
  • Maintain confidentiality of guest information

1.3 Customers

  • Provide accurate personal information
  • Notify system of changes to reservations
  • Adhere to payment deadlines

2. Data Management

2.1 Reservation data retention period: 5 years

2.2 Payment information is processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways

2.3 User profile deletion requests must be submitted in writing

3. Booking Regulations

  • Minimum stay duration: 24 hours
  • Deposit requirement: 30% of total booking cost
  • Cancellation window for full refund: 72 hours prior to check-in
  • No-show penalty: 50% of booking value

4. Service Terms

4.1 Room service availability: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM

4.2 Maintenance requests will be addressed within 2 hours of submission

4.3 Damages to property will incur repair fees plus 20% service charge

5. Security Protocols

  • Mandatory password rotation every 90 days
  • Session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity
  • Encrypted data transmission (AES-256)

6. Liability Limitations

6.1 The hotel is not responsible for:

  • Personal belongings left unattended
  • Third-party service interruptions
  • Force majeure events

7. Compliance Standards

  • GDPR Article 32 compliance
  • PCI DSS Level 1 Certification
  • Local tourism authority regulations

8. Policy Amendments

8.1 Updates will be communicated through:

  • System notification center
  • Registered email addresses
  • Posted notices in public areas
